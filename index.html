Our goals

As a tech company, Accomptant team is aiming to revolutionize the accounting and finance industry with data science/AI and automation. With our AI platform, softwares and applications, the burden of manual work for accountants will be lessened and the accounting service costs for companies will be reduced. We believe the advance of AI technologies will transform the accounting industry and improve the efficiency of accounting services.

Our platform leverages the advanced data science and AI technologies, tools and models to provide an end-to-end pipeline and innovative solutions for accounting service. We provide visualization, statistical insights, predictions and financial reports for the diverse financial datasets. We are aiming to provide applications that acheive the best performance and most friendly user experience. We are combining the data, coding and workflows to streamline the accouning service. Accomptant works towards providing a smart AI Agent for accounting.

About

Our History

Accomptant Inc. was founded in December, 2022. The company is located in New York City, New York. Accomptant is a tech company focusing on developing software products for accounting and finanical services. Accomptant has collaborated with accounting consulting company and accountants to provide innovative, data-driven, and automative solutions in bookkeeping, financial reporting, tax preparation and payroll services.

Our Logo

Our logo consists of elements of bytes (0/1), ancient Chinese Abacus, and flowcharts. This demonstrates that we are building products that combines data-driven approaches, computing and automation solutions in accounting (Chinese Abacus which is a counting tool and was popular tool for accountants in old days).
